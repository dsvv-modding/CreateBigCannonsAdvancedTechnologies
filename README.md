## This Mod is an addon for the well known Create Big Cannons (CBC) mod.

### In this mod are 110 new cannon blocks

*   20 Big Cannon Blocks
*   28 Projectile Blocks
*   6 Autocannon Blocks
*   30 Twin Autocannon Blocks
*   18 Heavy Autocannon Blocks
*   6 Rocket Pod Blocks
*   2 Medium Rocket Rail Blocks

### And there are new Items

*   4 new Autocannon Ammunitions
*   3 Heavy Autocannon Ammunitions
*   4 different Rockets
*   4 different Medium Rockets

In addition more blocks like unbored cannon blocks and cast moulds.

### New Cannons:

This mod adds four new cannon types. The  double barreld Autocannons (Twin Autocannon) and bigger caliber Autocannons (Heavy Autocannon). In addition there are Rocket Pods and Rocket Rails for bigger rockets

### New Ammunition:

Ever wanted to have a single muntition block that contains the projectile and the propellant in one? Now You can have it. There are two types. Weak ones that can be supported by additional propellant and strong ones that fail if they get more propellant.

# New Blocks:

Now we're comming to the highlight of it all! The cannon blocks.  
    

## \- Big Cannons:

### \- Muzzle Brake:

The Muzzle Brake reduces the recoil of the cannon with the pressure of the gas inside the barrel. Longer barrel means less pressure means less knockback reduction.

### \- Fume Extractor:

The Fume Extractor reduces the size of the particle cloud in front of the cannon. This can help you hiding from your target.

### \- Silencer:

Ever turned your game volume down because the Cannons fire so loud? I have a solution: The Silencer. It reduces recoil and noise of the Cannon.

### \- Rifled Barrel:

The Rifled Barrel is a variant of the normal barrel with same outer loock, but it decreases spread even more than the normal barrel.

## \- Twin Autocannon:
